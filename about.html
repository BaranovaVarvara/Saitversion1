About the Wooden Fair

Address and Contact

The Wooden Fair is located at 14k3 Brever Street, London. Buses run from Heathrow to the city, which cost 6 pounds. According to the time, the bus ride will be approximately 1 hour. You can also use the local subway, spend about 1 hour on the road and pay 5 pounds.There is another option to get to London by train. The train will cost 25 pounds, but the road will not be long, this type of transport will take you to the place in 15 minutes. For tourists who like to move comfortably and not carry suitcases, we suggest taking a taxi to the hotel or to the center of London. A taxi will take you in 40 minutes and will cost from 90 pounds.

Our partners

There is always a large selection of skirting boards, platbands, interior and exterior corners, box timber, dobory for window and door slopes.
